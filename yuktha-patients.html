Emergency QR Card

If something happens
to you — you're covered.

Every Yuktha patient gets a personal Emergency QR card. If you're in an accident or can't speak for yourself, anyone who scans it instantly sees your critical medical information — no password, no login, no delay.

1

Get your QR code

Yuktha generates a unique QR card for you the moment you sign up. Print it, save it on your phone, or add it to your wallet.

2

You choose what's visible

You decide which critical details to share publicly — blood group, allergies, emergency contact. Everything else is locked with a PIN, only for your doctor.

3

Scan in seconds, anywhere

Any smartphone, any hospital, any first responder — one scan, and the right people have the right information immediately.

🔒

Your privacy is protected. Only what you choose is public. Full history only with your PIN or your doctor's verified login. Every access is logged and you're notified.

Privacy & Security

Your data belongs
to you.

We are extremely careful with your health information. Here's exactly what we do to protect it.

🇮🇳

Stored only in India

Your health data never leaves India. Hosted on Indian servers, subject to Indian law, under your control.

🔒

You control what's shared

Nothing is public unless you turn it on, field by field. You can change or remove your Emergency QR data any time.

👁️

You see every access

Every time someone views your full records — a doctor, a hospital, anyone — you get notified immediately.

🚫

Never sold, never shared

Your data is never sold to insurers, pharma companies, or advertisers. Ever. That's a promise, not just a policy.
